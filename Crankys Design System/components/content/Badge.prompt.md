One-line: Small uppercase pill for flagging urgency, category or status — keep them rare so they carry weight.

```jsx
<Badge tone="urgent">Urgent appeal</Badge>
<Badge tone="green">Resident</Badge>
<Badge tone="warm" icon={<Paw/>}>Needs a home</Badge>
```

Tones: `green` (default) · `urgent` (raspberry) · `warm` (clay) · `sun` · `info` · `neutral` · `solid`. Sizes `sm|md`.
