One-line: Big serif figure + label for the impact strip ("how your support helps").

```jsx
<Stat value="500+" label="Animals rescued" sublabel="since 2010" />
<Stat value="15 yrs" label="Caring for farm animals" tone="clay" />
<Stat value="100%" label="Donations to animal care" tone="onDeep" />
```

Tones: `green` (default) · `clay` · `berry` · `onDeep` (for green/dark bands). `align`: `center|start`.
