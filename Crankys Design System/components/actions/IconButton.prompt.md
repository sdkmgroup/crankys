One-line: Icon-only button (circle or rounded) for nav, social, close, and toolbar actions — always give it an accessible `label`.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Open menu" variant="outline"><MenuIcon/></IconButton>
<IconButton label="Donate" variant="donate"><HeartIcon/></IconButton>
```

Variants: `soft` (default) · `primary` · `donate` · `outline`. Sizes `sm|md|lg` (md = 44px, meets touch targets). `shape` `circle` (default) or `rounded`.
